UX REVIEW WORKFLOW:

GOAL:
Ensure the interface is simple, clear, and user-friendly.

---

1. CLARITY
- Is everything understandable?
- Any confusion?

---

2. FLOW
- Is navigation smooth?
- Any unnecessary steps?

---

3. CONSISTENCY
- Same design patterns everywhere?

---

4. FEEDBACK
- Loading / error / success states present?

---

5. ACCESSIBILITY
- Readable text?
- Good contrast?

---

6. MOBILE EXPERIENCE
- Works well on small screens?

---

OUTPUT:

- Problems
- UX improvements
- Final rating (1-10)
