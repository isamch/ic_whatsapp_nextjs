ARCHITECTURE REVIEW:

GOAL:
Evaluate system design quality.

---

1. STRUCTURE
- Clear architecture?

---

2. SEPARATION
- Layers separated (UI / logic / data)?

---

3. SCALABILITY
- Can it grow?

---

4. MAINTAINABILITY
- Easy to modify?

---

5. COUPLING
- Too dependent parts?

---

OUTPUT:

- Weak points
- Improvements
- Final evaluation
